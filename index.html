Parcel Damage Classification

We embarked on a mission to address the challenge of identifying damaged packages with the goal of implementing an automated system within distribution centers to detect and flag damaged packages as they traverse through the process. In the course of our research, we delved into various models to ascertain the most effective machine learning model.
Our investigation revealed that a VGG model, enhanced with a custom top layer, delivered the optimal performance, boasting a high accuracy rate and recall rate. In the context of a warehouse environment, where the consequences of erroneously marking damaged packages as undamaged are less desirable than the reverse, a high recall rate holds particular significance.
